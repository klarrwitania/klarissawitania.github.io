About Me

Hi! Welcome to my portfolio. ICN Cultural Production

As the UI/UX designer for the ICN website, I worked on three key pages: the landing page, committee page, and ticketing page. This experience allowed me to further my knowledge of designing in Figma, particularly in advanced prototyping. For the landing page, I incorporated a parallax design to create a dynamic and engaging user experience. I taught myself how to implement this effect in Figma by watching tutorials, blending creativity with technical execution to design an interactive interface that encourages ticket purchases.

When working on the committee and ticketing pages, I learned about creating professional and well-organized designs. I refined my approach to presenting design elements and resources, such as color palettes and layout components, in a structured and efficient way. I also learned to standardize key design choices, like border and font sizes, ensuring a cohesive and polished look.

Overall, this project not only enhanced my design skills but also provided valuable insights into front-end development, giving me a clearer picture of how design translates into implementation.

NTUMods

NTUMODS is a non-profit, student-run initiative under the NTU Student’s Union Information Technology Committee, aimed at providing a more efficient platform for students to organize their school schedules. This academic year, the committee is enhancing the project with additional features. As the UI/UX designer, I am currently focused on designing the course feedback section interfaces using Figma. I look forward to contributing to future features while continuing to refine and expand my UI/UX skills.

Get Together Day

As part of the Information Technology Committee, I worked with a team of 16 to create the GTD (Freshmen Orientation Program) website, showcasing events and activities for participants. I was responsible for designing the UI/UX, using tools like Figma to create wireframes, icons, and interactive prototypes for an engaging and smooth user experience.

Through this project, I learned a lot about front-end and web development, working with frameworks and programming languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Next.js, ReactJS, Tailwind, and Git.

IE0005 - Introduction to Data Science & Artificial Intelligence


This machine learning project has been a great opportunity for me to deepen my understanding of key concepts in Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and machine learning models. Throughout the project, I gained valuable hands-on experience in data science. Working as a group of four, we were tasked with building machine learning models to address our problem statement. With a clear problem statement, I found the project to be particularly motivating because I had a goal in mind that I wanted to achieved. As a result, our model achieved a predictor accuracy of almost 90%, which was a great result for the team.
